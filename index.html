about

Our Beginnings and Growth

Akai Hana started as a small restaurant with only 40 seats in 1986, and quickly became known as a great place to get authentic Japanese food. Before long, we became so popular we moved into our present location, which seats 130. Now, thousands of loyal customers make Akai Hana a regular destination.

The Food – Outstanding!

We have a wonderful selection of Japanese favorites. Our tempura is always made fresh to order, lightly fried. The dinner box and lunch box is served in a lacquered box with a beautiful display of meat or fish, rice, and several traditional sides. There’s something for everybody to enjoy from Akai Hana’s large and varied menu.

Sushi – Our Pride

Akai Hana is well known for having superior sushi, made by our highly skilled, professionally trained sushi chefs. This is REAL sushi. Fish imported directly from Japan is available, and all our ingredients are guaranteed to be “super fresh”. Sushi lovers from New York and California tell us our sushi is among the best in America.

The Atmosphere

Our dining room is warm, comfortable and elegant, with beautiful shades of wood.You can always hear the sound of our guests talking and laughing and enjoying the Akai Hana experience. Our guests feel like family to us, and we’re confident that once you dine here, you’ll want to visit us again and again.

We look forward to seeing you soon!
